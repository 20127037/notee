Ultimate guideline to master using this application

1. Add new note

Simply need to click on the Add button in the navigation bar.
Afterwards, a modal will pop up prior to ask you fill in some information fields.
The final step is clicking on the Save button.

2. View full content of specific note

Click arbitrarily on a row to open its extended content.

3. Modify note

♦ Modify status

Make a click right on the table, cell that specifies the current status to toggle it.

♦ Modify content

Repeat the steps of the second guide.
After that, click on the Modify button and start to edit your note.

4. Delete note

Repeat the steps of the second guide.
Click on the Delete button to immediately delete the note.

5. Search for note

Type in any keywords (accept querying for both title and content), press Enter and the result will immediately be seen.

6. Filter the table

♦ Hide specific status

Click on the Filter button.
Hover the right arrow in order to view 2 available options.

♦ Arrange by time

Click on the Filter button.
Hover the right arrow in order to view 2 available options.
